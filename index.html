About the Film

The story of a man who helped redefine television.

Sock It To Me: The Legend of George Schlatter is a vivid portrait of the producer, director, and writer whose singular voice helped transform American entertainment. Through rare archival footage and intimate contemporary interviews, the film traces Schlatter’s extraordinary journey from television innovator to enduring cultural force.

At 96, Schlatter stands as one of the last living architects of television’s golden age — sharp, irreverent, inventive, and deeply human. The documentary celebrates not only his body of work, but the spirit behind it: risk-taking, wit, mischief, and a belief that comedy can move culture.
